## Welcome to SuperMemory

This quickstart guide will help you set up SuperMemory and add your first memories in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [supermemory.ai](https://supermemory.ai) and create your free account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your email and verify your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Setup">
    Follow the onboarding flow to set up your profile
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Install Browser Extension

The browser extension makes saving content effortless:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Chrome">
    1. Visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore)
    2. Search for "SuperMemory"
    3. Click "Add to Chrome"
    4. Sign in with your SuperMemory account
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Firefox">
    1. Visit [Firefox Add-ons](https://addons.mozilla.org)
    2. Search for "SuperMemory"
    3. Click "Add to Firefox"
    4. Sign in with your SuperMemory account
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Safari">
    1. Download from the App Store
    2. Enable in Safari Extensions
    3. Sign in with your SuperMemory account
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Add Your First Memory

Let's add some content to SuperMemory:

### Method 1: Browser Extension
1. Navigate to any webpage
2. Click the SuperMemory extension icon
3. Choose a space (or create a new one)
4. Click "Save"

### Method 2: Web Dashboard
1. Go to your [SuperMemory dashboard](https://supermemory.ai/dashboard)
2. Click the "+" button
3. Paste a URL or type/paste text content
4. Select the content type and space
5. Click "Add Memory"

### Method 3: API
```bash
curl -X POST https://api.vevibe.com/v1/add \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "type": "page",
    "content": "https://example.com/article",
    "spaces": ["your-space-id"]
  }'
```

## Step 4: Create Your First Space

Spaces help organize your knowledge:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Spaces">
    Go to the Spaces section in your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Space">
    Click "Create Space" and give it a name like "Learning" or "Work"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Permissions">
    Choose if it's private or if you want to invite collaborators
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content">
    Start adding memories to your new space
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 5: Try AI Search and Chat

Now that you have some content, let's explore SuperMemory's AI features:

### Search Your Memories
1. Use the search bar in your dashboard
2. Try natural language queries like:
   - "articles about machine learning"
   - "notes from last week's meeting"
   - "research on climate change"

### Chat with Your Knowledge
1. Click on the Chat feature
2. Ask questions about your saved content:
   - "What did I save about productivity?"
   - "Summarize my research on AI"
   - "Find connections between my saved articles"
