## What is SuperMemory?

SuperMemory is your AI-powered second brain that helps you save, search, and chat with your knowledge. Whether it's web pages, documents, notes, or tweets, SuperMemory intelligently organizes and makes your information instantly searchable and conversational.

## Use Cases

SuperMemory is perfect for:

- **Researchers** - Save and organize research papers, articles, and findings
- **Students** - Keep track of course materials, notes, and references
- **Content Creators** - Manage inspiration, references, and research materials
- **Professionals** - Organize work documents, meeting notes, and industry insights
- **Developers** - Save code snippets, documentation, and technical resources
